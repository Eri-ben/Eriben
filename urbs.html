URBS

A GUI for booking university rooms and other facilities.

About URBS

URBS (Short for University Room Booking System) is a UI Design I made for the final examination of the course Graphical Interfaces I attented at Chalmers. The exam did not offer any time to gather any data but was to be carried out by following design guidelines, primarily from the book About Face written by Cooper et. al (2014).

To my help, I utilized tons of divergent sketching which helped me thoroughly explore the design space. I also made a site map to keep track of all my functions and filters.

The final design was made in Adobe XD.

The task

Design a University scheduling and room booking system.
